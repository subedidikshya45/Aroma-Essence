Queries Answered

Frequently Clarified Specifics

Have operational queries regarding application profiles, longevity indices, shipping methods, or localized custom modifications?

What is the typical projection duration of your Eau de Parfums?

Our fine fragrance collections feature an industry-leading concentration factor of 18% to 23% pure oils. Under normal conditions across Rupandehi, standard projection holds between 6 to 9 full hours based on chemical interactions with individual skin oil profiles.

How should the Aroma Essences be optimally diffused?

For ultrasonic cool-mist humidifiers, mix 4 to 7 drops into 200ml of clean filtered water. For classic candle-heated oil burners, add 3 drops diluted into warm water directly atop the ceramic dish assembly.

Do you offer physical sampling before bulk corporate procurement?

Yes, absolutely. Clients are cordially welcome to visit our studio storefront layout directly in Butwal to sample everything. Alternatively, we dispatch physical discovery sample boxes via local express courier networks to your address.

Are your formulations skin-safe for sensitive dermatological profiles?

All AURA products rely completely on premium sugarcane alcohol vectors and organic scent extractions. We intentionally omit phthalates, synthetic chemical stabilizers, and sulfates. We advise a localized tiny inner wrist patch test prior to full daily usage.

What are your delivery parameters outside the Lumbini area?

We provide full courier delivery across all primary hubs within Nepal (including Kathmandu, Pokhara, Chitwan, Biratnagar, and Nepalgunj) with localized door delivery fulfilled within 2 to 4 business handling days.
